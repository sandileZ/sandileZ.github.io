Currency Converter

About Currency Conversion

Currency conversion lets you translate an amount from one currency to another based on the latest exchange rates.

How It Works

  1. Select your source and target currencies.
  2. Enter an amount and click Convert.
  3. Rates are fetched in real time from exchangerate.host.

API Reliability & Disclaimers

Rates are provided free by exchangerate.host. They update every few minutes, but for critical financial decisions always check with your bank or official FX provider.
